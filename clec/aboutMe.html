About me

Since a young age I've grown up messing with computers and gadgets, leading me to work with VOIP phones from the age of 12. Since then I had worked with my step-father Kurt Kaiser. During my childhood he would bring me on jobs and task me with learning how VOIP systems work and how they are programmed.

Once I graduated highschool I worked at one of our partners, Netexpress providing support and programming to new and existing customers. This gave me the exsperience with working on a PBX system as well as working with clients and mapping out how they would like their phone system.

Sadly, in 2022 Kurt was diagnosed with cancer, so I started to work for him full time, picking up the work he was unable to do. When Kurt passed away in 2023, I was left with the company and continuing to run it today.
