Welcome to e-meyer.com! I'm Evan Meyer, a soon-to-be graduate from Eastern Michigan University, earning my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in December 2024. I'm passionate about exploring the intersections of technology, creativity, and performance.

My interests span a wide array: racing, building and experimenting with software and hardware, and creating immersive 3D models and interactive games. I've worked on exciting projects like CampQuest, a MERN stack web app, and ventured into the world of motion capture for animated characters. You can find some of these experiments and projects featured here!

In addition to web and game development, I have experience as a technician, where I optimized communication systems for businesses across Michigan, and as the owner of C-LEC Inc., overseeing system installations and customer connectivity solutions.

Feel free to dive into my demos, and let's connect to explore more about my work and interests!
